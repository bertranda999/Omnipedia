Influence
In-game Description:
Influence can be increased by building influence structures on eligible planets, and increasing planetary availability with Charters.

Explanation:
Influence does not appear naturally on planets. Further, it requires special planning to build planets with significant Influence. Spend it on Zones, convening the Council, Voting, and other actions.
