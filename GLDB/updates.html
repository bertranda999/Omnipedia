3 Sep 2010What happened to my legion/friends?
A NEW legion system is now available that allows players to establish their own organized alliance and
invite specific players into it. Leaders can name their legion and have full control over its management
and structure. A legion can have up to 50 players in its roster.

Why the change?
The previous system required that new players invite a large number of facebook (sometimes random) friends in order to
best take advantage of the bonuses legions had to offer. Organized legions have also been requested for quite a long time, as a way to
make it easier for a larger number of players to work together, rather than a disorganized network
of networks. The new system also allows for many exciting new features to be added that were not possible
with the prior system.

What will happen to my existing group of friends?
A: Your facebook friends will be viewable in a new area called 'View Friends'. This will allow you
to track where your friends are and what legions they are in. You can also invite your friends
into your own legion if they have not already joined one. Galaxy Legion will also inform you when
you come across a friends' ship or planet in your travels, and you may attack them if you wish :)

What are the benefits of the new legion structure?
Legions offer many of the benefits of the prior system, and more:

* Legion members can share locations of planets and elite/boss enemy ships
* Legion members can alert for help against enemy players
* You can send artifacts and minerals to your legion and/or your friends
* Legion members can repair each others ships and help construct structures on planets
* players recieve a 2% bonus to planetary attack, defense, and cloak for each active legion member
* Legion members can communicate using a private comm system
* Legion leaders and officers can manage invitations and roster for their group

Can I still gift and send items to my friends?
Yes, posting an extra artifact gift on your facebook profile will still allow your friends to gather them.
You can also still send artifacts and minerals to your friends OR your legion members - its your choice.

Are there any other benefits to my large group of Galaxy Legion friends?
Having a large group of friends is a great place to start when forming a new legion or recruiting
into an existing one. There may be additional benefits added in the future!

Legion Structure
How are legions organized?
Legions have the following roles:
* Leader. The leader can change any information for the legion, invite members into the legion, promote/demote members, kick members, and disband the entire legion.
* Officer. An officer is allowed to invite other members into the legion, kick standard members, and post in the officers comm area.
* Member. A standard member has no special legion privileges other than to communicate in the standard comm area.

How do I add other members to my legion?
Members can request to be added by:

* Finding your legion from the legion search area or player link and requesting to join
* Clicking on a legion invite link

Leaders and Officers can accept as many invitations as they wish, up to the maximum legion size.

You can also directly invite friends that you wish to have join your legion. They will be notified
and have the option to accept the invite.

Can multiple legions ally together?
Currently, there are no diplomacy options between legions. Each is independent and an enemy of all others....

* It has reached level 2
* It it has not already been targeted / disabled recently
* The legion controlling the base does not have an active Non-Aggression Pact or Battle treaty with the legion performing the scan
* It is not the same base the legion performing the scan has targeted in their last lock attempt.

Once a target lock is established, the legion may invite other legions to join the same battle. The list of available legions that have active 'battle' treaties will appear below the target lock. 27 Aug 2016In-Game Friends:

With this update, we are finally replacing the deprecated Facebook-synched friends feature with in-game friends. Due to this change, you will need to request friends from within the game again, even if you were friends before this feature. Player Blocking:

We continually get a variety of complaints and feedback regarding player "bullying", and have decided to leave it up to the players to make decisions on who they would like to continue to play with... or not. We understand that although this is a game that involves player-vs-player battles, some interactions can be taken very personally, which can affect the enjoyment of the game as whole.

Players will now be able to block other ships from interacting with theirs, and vice versa. In order to prevent abuse, blocks have the following restrictions (subject to change):

* Blocks last for exactly one month (cannot be canceled during this time), though they can be renewed.
* The requesting player must not have initiated any offensive actions against the player they wish to block in the last 24 hours, AND
* The requesting player must have at least 10 offensive actions from the player they wish to block in the last 24 hours, OR 30 actions in a 7-day period.
* Players are limited to a maximum of 5 players blocked at any given time. To block another, one will have to expire first.

We hope that this change will help some players make better choices about how they interact with others, and foster an environment of greater respect despite the PVP nature of the game.

Note that comm blocking remains the same as before (ie - you can block a comm without blocking the entire player).

Players can manage friends and blocks in the updated 'Friends and Blocks' area of the Legion Tab. This replaces the older 'Blocked Players' area in ship settings.

Facebook Invites:

The game will now track other players you invite from outside of the game via Facebook, making it easier to find them, friend them, etc. You'll see a list of your invited friends and their status in the new Friends tab of the 'Friends and Blocks' area. We have also started to offer incentives for these invites - see some of the new medals for reference.

Note that friends you have invited prior to this release will unfortunately not be tracked or credited in this way. Only friends invited via the 'Invite more Friends to Play' area are tracked.
